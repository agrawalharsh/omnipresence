About Us

Omnipresence Traders Omnipresence Traders is an international trading company. We buy and sell all types of scrap and steel related products domestically and internationally like Flat Steel, Long Steel,Tubular Steel, Engineering Steel, Semi-Finished Steel, Ferrous and Non-Ferrous Steel Scrap. We buy and sell all grades and specifications of Tinplate, Tin Free Steel, Tin Mill Black Plate, Galvanized Steel, Pre-Painted Steel, HMS 1 & 2, Stainless Steel Scrap, Aluminium Scrap. Omnipresence Traders is promoted by a group of experienced professionals. who after recognizing the growing demand of steel, scrap and related products at a rapid pace considering the industrialization and infrastructural development worldwide decided to establish Omnipresence Traders. Our business philosophy is to make the needs of our customers our main priority and we value relationships over profits. It is our mission to provide our customers with fast turnaround, exceptional quality, unparalleled customer service and competitive pricing, and to establish and maintain long term relationships with both the producers and the consumers, finding profitable solutions for all prime as well as non-prime steel. The team at Omnipresence Traders is driven to serve the customers as effectively and efficiently as possible and has pledged to: Understanding the customers’ business requirement
  • Dedication to professionalism
  • Reliability
  • Partnerships based on trust
  • Responsibility for satisfactory business transactions
    • We are committed to increase our presence in the international market by bringing more of value- added services and transparency in bulk steel procurement and serve our customers’ steel requirements on recurring basis.
